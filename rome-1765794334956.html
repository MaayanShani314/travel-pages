Exploring the Eternal City: Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and culture, known for its stunning architecture, ancient ruins, and delicious cuisine. Visitors can wander through its historic streets and discover landmarks that have shaped the course of history.

Top Attractions

Colosseum

The iconic symbol of Rome, the Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater that once hosted gladiatorial contests and public spectacles. It is an architectural marvel and a UNESCO World Heritage site.
